2. Career

Taylor Swift began her career as a country singer when she was still a teenager. Her early albums quickly gained popularity, especially among young listeners.

As her career developed, she gradually transitioned from country music to mainstream pop. Her album "1989" marked a major turning point, establishing her as a global pop superstar.

Over the years, she has continued to experiment with different styles, including indie and alternative sounds. Her ability to reinvent herself while maintaining her storytelling style has helped her remain relevant in the music industry.
